About

StockWare is an online platform that allows users to share applications across various platforms (Android, Windows, etc.).

Objective

For many independent developers, it can be challenging to distribute their creations in a simple and accessible way. Major platforms like the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store offer visibility, but at significant costs. While it's possible to generate direct links to an application, these links are often temporary and unreliable. This is where StockWare comes in: it enables developers to share their applications without worrying about costs or managing expiring links. Only one link is needed to ensure the distribution of your app via this website.

Terms of Use

All applications available on StockWare are thoroughly verified. They are free to download but may include in-app purchases or paid features.
