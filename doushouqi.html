Explanation of Application Functionality

This application is an offline mobile board game. Two players compete by each controlling an army of 7 animals, with the goal of capturing the opponent’s base. You will find, by launching the game, the complete rules, records of previous game scores, and application settings. Currently, 7 languages are available: English (default), French, German, Spanish, Italian, Ukrainian, and Chinese.
